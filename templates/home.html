The  Challenge

  • Manual attendance is tedious and time consuming.
  • Teachers spend their precious teaching time in administrative tasks.
  • It is inherently vulnerable to proxies and manual errors.

The  Solution

DAIICT Attendance is an AI powered face recognition solution which uses Computer Vision and Machine Learning algorithms to mark the attendance of the students of the Institute.

The system will work on face recognition where each student in the class will be photographed and their details will be stored in a server. The faculty can then record the attendance by just adding some details of class. On just one click CCTV camera of the class will take video frame of class. The system will recognize the faces and verify the presence or absence of each student.

How it  Works

The system works on face recognition where each student in the class is photographed and their details are stored in a dataset. The teacher can then record the attendance by inserting class details. On a single click the system will recognize the faces and verify the presence or absence of each student. Students can view their attendance through DAIICT Attendance Portal.
